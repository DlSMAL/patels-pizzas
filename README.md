# 🍕 Patel's Pizzas

A pizza restaurant game — playable right in your browser!

## Features

- **Animated background** — colourful gradient shifts and floating pizza emojis
- **Live pizza menu** — bake and sell 11 different recipes with real-time countdown timers
- **🌟 Patel's Paneer Special** — Mr. Patel's personal signature pizza, available from day one
- **Upgrade Shop** — improve your oven, hire workers, boost quality, marketing, delivery and more
- **Custom Pizza Builder** — choose your base, sauce and up to 6 toppings, then watch a step-by-step animation
- **Topping unlock system** — sell enough menu pizzas to unlock premium toppings (including Paneer!)
- **Achievements** — 14 achievements to earn, including *Patel's Favourite* for selling his Paneer pizza
- **Save / Load** — progress is saved to your browser's localStorage

## How to Play

Just open `index.html` in any modern browser — no installation required.

## Gameplay

### Menu Pizzas
1. Click any pizza card to start baking.
2. Watch the progress bar in the oven display.
3. Money is collected automatically when baking finishes.
4. Upgrade **Workers** to bake multiple pizzas at once.

### Mr. Patel's Paneer Special 🌟
- Available from the very first day — it's Mr. Patel's personal request!
- Sell 3 to unlock the **Paneer** topping in the Custom Pizza Builder.
- Sell 5 to earn the **Patel's Favourite** achievement and a cash reward.

### Custom Pizza Builder
1. Click **🍕 Make Your Own Pizza** from the game screen.
2. Choose a **base**, **sauce**, and up to **6 toppings**.
3. Watch the live step-by-step animation as your pizza is made!
4. Sell menu pizzas to unlock higher-tier toppings.

### Upgrades
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| 🔥 Oven | −30% bake time per level |
| 👨‍💼 Workers | +1 oven slot per level |
| ⭐ Quality | +15% sell price per level |
| 📢 Marketing | +10% sell price per level |
| 📦 Storage | Unlocks more menu recipes |
| 🚚 Delivery | +$2 bonus per sale per level |
| 📖 Recipe Mastery | +20% custom pizza value |
| 🎁 Loyalty | +5% chance of double earnings |
