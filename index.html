📖 How to Play

OBJECTIVE

Build the most successful pizza shop empire!

GAMEPLAY

  1. Order pizzas from the menu (1-7)
  2. Watch them bake in real-time
  3. Collect money when they're ready
  4. Invest in upgrades to increase profits

MENU CODES

  • [1-7] Bake a pizza
  • [U] Open upgrade shop
  • [S] View statistics
  • [A] View achievements
  • [P] Pause game

UPGRADES

  • 🔥 Oven - Reduces bake time
  • 👨‍💼 Workers - Increases oven capacity
  • ⭐ Quality - Increases pizza prices
  • 📢 Marketing - Boosts prices through reputation
  • 📦 Storage - Store more ingredients
  • 🚚 Delivery - Earn a bonus on every pizza sold
  • 📖 Recipe Mastery - Boosts your custom pizza sell value
  • 🎁 Loyalty Program - Chance of double earnings on each sale

CUSTOM PIZZA BUILDER 🍕

  • Click 🍕 Make Your Own Pizza from the game screen
  • Choose a base, sauce and up to 6 toppings
  • The ingredient cost is deducted from your money upfront
  • Watch a step-by-step animation as your pizza is made!
  • Sell your custom pizza and pocket the profit
  • Toppings unlock by selling enough of specific menu pizzas — sell basic pizzas first to unlock premium toppings!

TIPS

  • Higher difficulty = better rewards!
  • Upgrades multiply your profits
  • Random bonuses appear frequently
  • Check achievements for rewards!
  • Sell lots of basic pizzas to unlock premium toppings for the builder
